Welcome to GoEV

A car-share scheme offering hourly and daily hire of 100% electric cars and vans to the Isles of Scilly community.

Sign Up
Log In

GoEV Car Share is a simple, low cost and environmentally friendly alternative to owning your own car or van. You only pay for the time you use our vehicles, and can pick and choose the right vehicle for the job whenever you need one.

How it works

Become a member

Sign up to GoEV Car Share, complete your driver profile and receive your membership card.

Make a booking

Search for a vehicle and find the van, car or combi at a convenient location around St Mary's. Pick the date and time you want to use it, and accept the all inclusive price.

Collect your vehicle

Go to your chosen vehicle, and unlock it using your membership card on the card reader, unplug the charger, and drive away!

Return your vehicle

When it's time to return your vehicle, take it back to its home charger, plug it in and lock it using your membership card. That's it!

Frequently asked questions

What vehicles are available?
We have a mixture of Nissan Leaf cars, Nissan eNV-200 vans and Nissan eNV-200 Combi people carriers.
Where do I collect the vehicle from?
Each vehicle has a "home" chargepoint, where you will collect and return it from. When making your booking, you'll be able to select a vehicle at a convenient location.
Do I have to pay for fuel?
No - all electricty usage is included in the hire charge.
How often will I have to charge the vehcile?
In typical Scilly usage, all our vehicles last at least 100 miles on a charge, so you are unlikely to need to charge the vehicle during your hire.
I'm hiring a vehicle for a long time, what happens if I run out of charge?
If you are running low on charge during your hire, you can return your vehicle to it's home charge point to recharge it at any time. Simply plug it in and press the "charge now" button on the charger.
What are the conditions for drivers to sign up?
You must be a permanent resident of the Isles of Scilly or a contractor working for a business located on the Isles of Scilly, be between the ages of 21 and 70, with no mnore than 6 points on your driving license.
